Deterministic topic hash from match name. Race-free room creation.

Layer 2

Pure reducer linearises every writer into one ordered view.

Layer 3

QVAC Local AI

Streams live commentary from match events. Sovereign & private.

Split-brain healing: peers auto-migrate to the smallest discovery-key room.
